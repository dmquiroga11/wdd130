Total Rafting Plans

Rafting is a sporting discipline that reinforces self-esteem and helps us face our fears. Getting on a raft and launching yourself through the imposing rapids of a river is a true act of bravery. After having made a descent, the problems that we have to face daily will no longer seem so important to us. After having had an extreme feeling like this, a new perspective is acquired that strengthens us and allows us to face problems from a much more objective point of view.

History

We are the first company that navigated its waters for the first time in an inflatable boat. In these years our operations have been built on the basis of a personalized service, with security policies that protect our clients and with standards of the highest quality. It is for this reason that we have the best team of guides, internationally certified and endorsed operating teams, the best adventure scenarios where nature and culture have always been protagonists.
